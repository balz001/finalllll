Грамматика для урока

Grammar Rules:

1. Zero Conditional:

Structure: If + present simple, present simple

Usage: Used for general truths, scientific facts, and situations that are always true.

Example: If you heat water to 100 degrees, it boils.

2. First Conditional

Structure: If + present simple, will + base form

Usage: Used for real and possible situations in the future.

Example: If it rains, we will stay indoors.

3. Second Conditional

Structure: If + past simple, would + base form

Usage: Used for unreal or unlikely situations in the present or future.

Example: If I won the lottery, I would travel around the world.

4. Third Conditional

Structure: If + past perfect, would have + past participle

Usage: Used for unreal or unlikely situations in the past.

Example: If she had studied harder, she would have passed the exam.

Примечание: за несколько дней до урока также можно прислать ученику эти правила для повтора или изучения, а на урока отработать тему.

Подготовиться к отбору

1. Warm-up

a.    Discuss the following questions:

1. What emotions do you experience most frequently?
2. Can you think of a situation that triggered a strong emotion recently?

b. Watch this video and discuss the main idea.

2. Vocabulary

a. Match the emotions with their definitions:

  1. Happiness a. A feeling of great joy or pleasure.
  2. Anger b. A state of being upset or annoyed.
  3. Sadness c. The feeling of being afraid or frightened.
  4. Surprise d. A strong feeling of displeasure and belligerence.
  5. Fear e. A sudden and unexpected event.
Answers: 1-a, 2-d, 3-b, 4-e, 5-c.

b. Working with phrases and Idioms.

Примечание: в списке слов, который был дан для самостоятельного изучения, есть категория по идиомам и устойчивым выражениям. Можно поработать с ней отдельно в рамках урока.

Работа со списком включает 2 направления:
o Соединить фразу с ее значением
o Составить 2-3 предложения с наиболее понравившимися фразами.

3. Reading

Read the following text and answer the questions:

Emotions are complex reactions that involve psychological and physiological responses. They are often triggered by external stimuli or internal thoughts. The process of forming emotions begins in the brain, where signals are sent to various parts of the body. These signals result in physical changes, such as an increased heart rate or the release of certain hormones. Emotions play a crucial role in human communication and are essential for building connections with others.

Answer the questions:

  1. What triggers emotions?
  2. How do emotions affect the body?
  3. Why are emotions important in human communication?

4. Writing

Write a short paragraph describing a personal experience that evoked a strong emotion. Focus on the event, your feelings, and how you dealt with it. Use new words from the list.

Примечание: короткое эссе должно включать 10 предложений максимум. В работу должны быть включены новые слова из урока, идиома или устойчивое выражение. На работу следует отвести 5–7 минут. После этого педагог проверяет эссе вместе с учащимся, поправляя стиль и ход изложения мысли.

  • If you mix blue and yellow, you get green.
  • If she had known, she would have come to the party.
  • If he asks for help, I will assist him.

Task 2: Complete the Sentences

Complete each sentence with the correct form of the verb in parentheses.

  • If I __________ (have) a car, I would drive to work.
  • If it __________ (rain) tomorrow, we will cancel the picnic.
  • If she __________ (study) harder, she would have passed the exam.
  • If you __________ (ask) me, I would have helped you.
  • If the sun __________ (shine), we can go to the beach.

rains
had studied
had asked
shines
